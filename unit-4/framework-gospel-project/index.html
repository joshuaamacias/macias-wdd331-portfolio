Core themes

Short summaries + references (accurate, not reworded).

◎

Beatitudes: the “blessed” life

The Beatitudes describe who Christ calls “blessed” and what God promises.

Reference: Matthew 5:3–12

✶

Salt & light

Discipleship is visible. Your life can preserve, bless, and point others to God.

Reference: Matthew 5:13–16

☼

Higher law of the heart

Jesus moves from external compliance to inward transformation: desire, intent, and love.

Reference: Matthew 5:21–48

◈

Prayer & trust

Christ teaches sincere prayer, forgiveness, and confidence in the Father’s care.

Reference: Matthew 6:5–15, 25–34

⬡

Judging righteously

Start with self-examination, then help others with humility and clarity.

Reference: Matthew 7:1–5

⬒

Build on the rock

Hearing isn’t enough—Christ invites action, creating stability through obedience.

Reference: Matthew 7:24–27

Study guide

Tabs = quick navigation. Each tab includes a question + a simple application.

Blessed are…

Matthew 5:3–12

Focus

Study question

Which “blessed” description feels most needed in your life right now—and why?

Small application

  • Pick one Beatitude and write one sentence about how to live it today.
  • Do one quiet act of kindness with no credit.

Visible discipleship

Matthew 5:13–16

Practice

Study question

What does it mean to be “salt” and “light” without becoming performative?

Small application

  • Choose one relationship where you can “add light” (encourage, serve, reconcile).
  • Do it simply, without announcing it.

Prayer, forgiveness, and trust

Matthew 6:5–15, 25–34

Mindset

Study question

What worry is taking the most space in your mind—and what would it look like to place it with God?

Small application

  • Write a one-line prayer about a real concern (honest, specific).
  • Identify one next right step you can take today.

3-day reading plan

Accordion component. Short, realistic, and repeatable.

  • Read: Matthew 5:1–16
  • Underline: one promise
  • Do: one act of quiet goodness

  • Read: Matthew 5:17–48
  • Notice: how Jesus deepens the commandment
  • Do: one reconciliation step (even small)

  • Read: Matthew 6:5–15, 25–34; Matthew 7:24–27
  • Write: one worry + one next step
  • Do: one small act of obedience today

About this project

This page is a small gospel-themed study project (BYU-Idaho style) using one framework: Bootstrap.

  • Responsive layout using the grid
  • Components: navbar, cards, tabs, accordion, modal, toast
  • Custom CSS (colors/typography/effects) so it doesn’t look default
  • JavaScript: theme toggle, reflection save, copy-to-clipboard

You can expand this into multiple pages later (e.g., topics.html).
